Project Title
Salone HealthConnect: A Digital Healthcare Directory & Advisory Solution

Project Overview
Salone HealthConnect is a fully functional, mobile-responsive web platform designed to address critical healthcare accessibility challenges in Sierra Leone. The platform serves as a centralized digital hub where patients, caregivers, and community health workers can find verified medical facilities, book doctor appointments, coordinate blood and medical supply donations, and access emergency health information — all in one place, optimized for low-data environments.
Problem Statement
Sierra Leone has made significant progress in expanding healthcare infrastructure, yet millions of citizens still struggle to access basic health information when they need it most. The core challenges are:

Fragmented Information — Hospital contact details, opening hours, and available services are scattered across paper directories, word of mouth, and outdated resources with no single reliable source.
Emergency Delays — In life-threatening situations, finding the nearest open facility or reaching a blood donor can take hours, directly costing lives.
Rural-Urban Divide — Patients in districts such as Bo, Kenema, Kono, Makeni, and Tonkolili have little visibility into what specialist care is available and where.
Blood Shortage Coordination — Blood banks regularly run critically low with no public-facing system to alert donors or match urgent requests in real time.
High Data Costs — Most existing digital health tools are built for high-bandwidth environments, making them inaccessible to the majority of Sierra Leoneans using mobile data on 2G or 3G networks.

Solution
Salone HealthConnect bridges these gaps by providing a single, low-data-capable platform that brings together the four most critical healthcare needs into one accessible website:
Blood & Medical Donation Network

A tabbed donation platform covering blood donation, medicine contributions, medical equipment, and financial support. The blood section displays live stock levels by blood type at major facilities, allows users to select their blood type, and links them to a donor registration form. All donation categories connect facilities in greatest need with willing contributors across the country.
Emergency Information Access

Emergency contact numbers — including the national emergency line 999 and ambulance line 117 — are prominently displayed on every page through a persistent banner and a floating emergency button, ensuring no user ever has to search for critical contacts in a crisis.
Technical Approach

Built with pure HTML and CSS for maximum compatibility and speed
JavaScript handles interactivity — search filtering, time slot selection, tab switching, modal, and mobile navigation
Google Maps Embed API integrated on the hospital search page for location-based facility discovery
Designed mobile-first with full responsiveness down to 320px screen width
Low-data optimized — no heavy frameworks, no large image files, no unnecessary external dependencies
All pages share a single style.css stylesheet built on CSS custom properties for easy theming and consistency
Impact & Vision
Salone HealthConnect exists to ensure that no life in Sierra Leone is lost simply because someone could not find the nearest hospital, did not know which doctor to see, or could not reach a blood donor in time. By centralizing verified healthcare information in a low-cost, mobile-friendly platform, the project directly supports Sierra Leone's national health goals and lays the foundation for a fully connected healthcare ecosystem — one that serves every citizen, from Freetown to the most remote community in Kono or Tonkolili.
